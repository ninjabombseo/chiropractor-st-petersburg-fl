Chiropractic has actually come a long way ...
In 1895, Daniel David Palmer, the father of contemporary chiropractic, performed the very first chiropractic modification on a patient of his who had lost his hearing after a back injury some 17 years previously. The patient reported that after Dr. Palmer adjusted a vertebra in his upper back, a few of his hearing returned.
In its infancy, chiropractic was thought about part approach, part art, and part science. Although the practice had actually been around for centuries, nobody-- before Palmer, that is-- had actually drawn connections in between the how and why of spine control.
For the next 120+ years, chiropractic care has actually grown and developed into what it is today: a worldwide, recognized treatment for all types of skeletal pain and disorders, and an alternative method of treatment for lots of other conditions.
